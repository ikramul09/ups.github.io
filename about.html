Teachers Day


Good morning to all the teachers and my dear friends. The light of the world, the beacon in the dark and the hope that gives us strength to survive, is our teacher. Today we celebrate Teachers' Day. A day, kept aside to honour the gifted souls who work everyday to make sure that the future is bright for all of us. Let us welcome all the teachers with a big round of applause. On this beautiful occasion, let us take the opportunity to convey our wishes to all our teachers, who have given impeccable contribution in shaping us. Every year 5th of September, we celebrate Teachers Day. It is a day filled with lots of excitement, joy and happiness as students are eagerly looking forward to tell their teachers how and why are they special to them. It is my honour to to talk about our dear teachers on this wonderful occasion. We celebrate Teachers' day on 5th of September every year in India. September 5th is marked by the birth anniversary of Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan and the Teachers' day is celebrated in commemoration of his birthday. Along with being a succesful leader in the form of the President of the Country, Dr. Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan was great scholar and an excellent teacher.

Republic Day


This year, India celebrates its 71st Republic Day — commemorating the historic moment when India became an independent republic country and the constitution of India came into effect. Republic Day is near, and our country is all set for the grand celebration on January 26. This year, India celebrates its 71st Republic Day — commemorating the historic moment when our nation became an independent republic country. Republic Day honors the day when the constitution of India came into effect. Every school across the country is gearing up for the Republic day celebration on January 26. Various competitions are being held at schools and speech competition is no exception. If your little one is participating in the speech competition, then a simple and eloquent speech is something that you would be in need of. To help you out, here is a sample speech for your kid to be a star on the Republic day celebration. Republic Day Speech Dear principal, teachers and friends As we all know we have gathered here for the Republic Day celebration. I am excited and glad to get a chance to say something about our country. This year, we are celebrating 71st republic day of our country. We celebrate republic day on January 26th, the day when the constitution of India came into effect. I am proud to be a citizen of India which ensures the fundamental rights of every citizen such as right to equality, freedom, religion, culture, education, privacy etc. On this auspicious occasion, let’s bow our heads before the memories of India’s freedom fighters and great leaders. I would like to wind up my speech followed by a moment of silence for those great souls. This is an occasion to reaffirm our commitment to unity in diversity, fraternity and equality among all our citizens. Thanks again for giving me an opportunity to speak in front of you all. Jai Hind
